Stallcup's List

A Proposal for Merit-Based Immigration Recognition

Recently, President Trump expressed interest in identifying potential migrants who should be allowed to stay in the United States of America. What a beautiful olive branch to extend. President Trump is a merciful leader, even when some people disrespect our country by looting and burning our flag.

Our Commander In Chief understands that some migrants are actually family oriented, Jesus loving, honest workers who contribute to our farms, hospitality and industries. You know, the ones with no criminal records who love our country and believe in the American Dream.

May I respectfully suggest starting a STALLCUP'S LIST to help Trump identify the migrants in our country who deserve a chance. More than just workers on farms, in hotels and other service industry jobs... These are the people who contribute to our communities and respect our great beautiful country. They hold up the American Flag with all of their heart.

This list would not grant citizenship but offer immunity to those honest migrants who have earned their place here through sweat and integrity. It's not about opening the gates wide, it's about identifying the good migrants who love America.
